What are Mutual Funds ?

Mutual funds are a diverse investment option, pooling funds from multiple investors to buy a diversified portfolio of stocks, bonds, or other securities. They offer investors professional management, liquidity, and diversification, making them a popular choice for those seeking a hands-off approach to investing. While they can provide potential for growth, it's important to assess your risk tolerance and investment goals before investing. Research and choose funds that align with your objectives, as fees and returns can vary. Always consult with a financial advisor to create a well-rounded investment strategy. Mutual funds offer an accessible entry point to the world of investing, catering to both beginners and experienced investors.
